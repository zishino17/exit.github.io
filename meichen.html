Meichen Liu is a printmaker and designer based in Providence, Rhode Island, USA and Dalian, China. Recently, her major interest is exploring the possibilities of how mathematics and art can be paired together. She works with graphite, watercolor, and computer languages such as c++. Her works have been presented in several exhibitions, such as FT-collaboration in Shanghai, RISD unbound at Rhode Island School of Design (Providence).
